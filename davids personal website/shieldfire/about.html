Our Story

Dedicated to Fire Safety Since 1999

ShieldFire Protection was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, code-compliant fire protection services that building owners can trust. What started as a small team of dedicated technicians has grown into one of the region's most trusted fire protection companies.

Over the past 25 years, we've completed more than 10,000 inspections across hundreds of commercial, industrial, and residential properties. Our team of NICET-certified technicians brings deep expertise in fire sprinkler systems, fire pumps, and all related fire protection equipment.

We believe that fire safety shouldn't be complicated. That's why we make the process simple — from scheduling to inspection to report delivery — so you can focus on running your business while we take care of your fire protection needs.
